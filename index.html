Our Story

Born & Raised in
Michigan's Deer Country

Michigan Quality Deer Habitat was started in 2016 out of a simple conviction: the best hunting comes from the best land stewardship. We didn't come out of a conference room or a consulting firm — we came out of the hardwoods, the swamp edges, and the ridge-tops that make Michigan one of the finest whitetail states in the country.

We work with Michigan landowners — family parcels, hunting clubs, and larger tracts alike — who want to take their property seriously. Whether your ground is overgrown, underperforming, or just needs a plan, we bring hands-on experience, straight talk, and a deep respect for the land to every property we step foot on.

Nearly a decade in, we've helped transform dozens of Michigan properties into thriving habitat for whitetail deer and native wildlife. No cookie-cutter plans. Every acre is different, and every plan we write is built around your ground, your goals, and your budget — nothing more, nothing less.

This is what we love to do. It shows in every food plot we put in, every timber stand we improve, and every satisfied landowner who watches their herd — and their hunting — get better year after year.

What We Do

Habitat & Herd Services

♦

Habitat Assessment & Planning

We walk your ground, read the terrain, and build a clear picture of what your property has — and what it needs. Every project starts here. No assumptions, no guesswork.

Food Plot Design & Installation

From site selection and soil prep to species selection and planting day, we design food plots that hold deer and keep them coming back throughout the season.

Timber Stand Improvement

Strategic tree removal and canopy management to increase mast production, open up browse, and create the diverse forest structure deer rely on all year long.

Hinge Cutting & Bedding Areas

Purpose-built bedding cover using hinge cutting and native structure to give deer — especially mature bucks — the security they're looking for on your land.

Deer Herd Management Plans

Harvest strategy, age structure goals, and doe management — tailored to your property size, herd data, and long-term vision for growing quality whitetails year after year.

Property Maintenance

From trail maintenance and water control structures to annual food plot management and property upkeep — we keep your land working hard season after season.
